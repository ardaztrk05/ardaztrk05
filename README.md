## About Me

I'm a 2nd-year Artificial Intelligence Engineering undergraduate at **Trabzon University**, combining academic work with hands-on software development, technical project management, and team leadership.

- 🚀 Founder & Team Leader of **İMEJE** — regional runner-up & national finalist at the TUA Astro Hackathon
- 🤖 President of the **AI & Robotics Club** at Trabzon University
- 🏥 Captain of **AuraMed**, competing in TEKNOFEST Health in AI category
- 🌐 Operations Manager, Business Development & Video Manager at **Huawei Student Developers (HSD)**
- 🧠 Building focused engineering projects: **NeuroSync AI**, **MetaHeart**, **Polaris**, and a **TÜBİTAK 2209-A** supported AI-driven ECG analysis project

My goal is to lead AI-driven initiatives by combining a strong technical background with operational and organizational leadership skills.
